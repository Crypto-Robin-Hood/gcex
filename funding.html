Funding Methods

We accept deposits and withdrawals through Visa and Mastercard, wire transfers, and fiat and cryptocurrency transactions.

Wire transfers: You can easily deposit to your account via bank transfer. We let you choose from accounts in 3 major currencies (US Dollars, Euros & Great British Pounds) so that you always get the best terms possible. Bank transfers are particularly useful for deposits of $1000 or more.

Credit cards: We accept major credit cards including Visa, Visa Debit & Mastercard. Quickly and securely fund your account with no fees or commissions.

Cryptocurrency deposit: Deposit your bitcoin, ethereum and litecoin directly into your trading account with zero conversion charges and fees!
