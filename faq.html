What is bitcoin?

Bitcoin is not a company. Bitcoin is a technology that facilitates the digital transfer of value. Bitcoin uses a global digital ledger that proves who owns what bitcoin at any given time. Bitcoin allows you to send any amount of value to anyone in the world, instantly, without the need of a trusted third party such as a bank or company.

What is the verification process?

We need to verify your identity to prevent fraud and money laundering. By doing so we create a safe environment for our customers to trade with each other. To open an account and start trading, we require your full name, date of birth, address, phone number, and passport details. How do I deposit funds?

After you have verified your account, visit your Global Crypto Exchange Wallet to view your legal tender and digital currency balances. From there you can create a deposit ticket for each deposit. You will receive an email with the deposit instructions so that you can submit a deposit from your bank account. If you are depositing digital currencies, such as bitcoin, you will be presented with a public address and QR Code to send the funds to. You will see the funds in your Global Crypto Exchange Account Balance once the funds are confirmed on the blockchain.

How do I withdraw funds?

Withdrawing funds is easy. Visit your Global Crypto Exchange Wallet to see your balance. From there select withdraw and enter your bank information for legal tender withdrawals, or your digital currency public address for digital currency withdrawals.
