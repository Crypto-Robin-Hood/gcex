It is very important that any personal information you provide is accurate and complete and relates to you only. We will never ask you to provide us with information about someone else.

For example, we may record information such as the areas of our Site that you visit, and the time, date and URL of the pages you access, your IP address and / or the previous website that you visited before linking to the Site. We provide more information about this below in the sections headed “Use of Cookies and data analytics” below.
